Temple Inn Reception Center

The Temple Inn Reception Center can be reserved for use for both temple wedding receptions as well as to house the wedding luncheon. The Reception Center is a beautiful open ballroom that can hold up to 500 people. It's open floorplan can easily be customized to meet the needs of the wedding party and guests.

Temple Inn Catering

We offer a simple catering menu for wedding luncheons priced per plate. The Reception Center can seat up to 150 people and can easily be customized to meet the needs of the wedding party.
